Bleeding Ink

By Sisa Manzini

Amnesia

I'm in ahouse with no windows
Only God knows the pain that it carries

These halls are made of prison walls
In a house not built for suvivors

The inhabitants are scared of receiving
the kind of torture that could awakend the dead

In the heart of the captor lies the answer
to their misery

But there's no regret from the source
why; it is a mystery
just drink up forget, froget and just like
that there's no pain left.

Forget the sins of yesterday, those that
we've washed away
So we can relive the torture

The sorrow of infinite tomorrow
Each day feels like new
Only the captor knew what yesterday had
held for you

Can you stand the life of despair where
the truth is in your nightmares

One day the light found a way and shone
a ray; that was really hope

And I say to the captor
It's time to take back tomorrow
You stole the future from the innocent
Now this is the price you pay
I take your life away

And this all happened in the light of day
